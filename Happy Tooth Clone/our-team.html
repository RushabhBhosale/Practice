Sarah Handy, RDH - Hygienist

Hi! My name is Sarah. I grew up all the way up North in Logan, Utah. After graduating from Sky View High School I traveled down to SLC to play soccer for Salt Lake Community College where I completed dental hygiene school. After two years of learning all about teeth and oral hygiene I graduated with Sigma Phi Alpha - National Dental Hygiene Honors with awards from Western Society of Periodontology and the Golden Scaler.

I worked at Happy Tooth as an assistant through hygiene school and am STOKED to be back as a dental hygienist! Youll find me shredding the slopes during the winter and boating in the sun during the summer. I also love fostering kitties, cooking pasta dinners, and watching movies with my husband. I cant wait to meet you at Happy Tooth and clean teeth!
